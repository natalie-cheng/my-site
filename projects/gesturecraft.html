Gesturecraft

September 2023 - June 2024 | Technological Innovations for Inclusive Learning & Teaching Lab (TIILT)

Gesturecraft is an ongoing project within the TIILT lab on which I work software developer and researcher. Our research goal is to develop an adaptive gesture-based interface to allow accessible play of Minecraft.

#team | #python | #machine-learning #mediapipe | #research

On this project, we created a controller system of 15+ customizable hand gestures to adaptively play Minecraft. Using Google's MediaPipe we trained an ML system to identify and classify different hand gestures. I also led the transfer from a local-based Python script to a browser-based Javascript application and collaborated with students at local Chicago middle schools, utilizing their feedback to develop and improve Gesturecraft.
