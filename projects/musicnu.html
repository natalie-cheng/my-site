MusicNU

April 2023 - Present | Technological Innovations for Inclusive Learning & Teaching Lab (TIILT)

MusicNU is an ongoing, entirely undergraduate-led project within the TIILT lab on which I work as a frontend researcher and developer. Our goal is to develop an interactive website that takes in a user-chosen song, performs melody, chord, and rhythm extraction. Using this analysis, we hope to create visualizations to help teach music theory in local high schools.

#team | #react.js | #node.js | #research

Currently, we are focused on further developing the frontend visualizations, and eventually incoporating them with the backend music analysis. In previous quarters, we worked on overarching web designs, as well as setting up our EC2 server.

When I joined this research team, I had no formal frontend experience. However, I knew that it was something I was interested in exploring, and I was excited to learn on the job.
